What the Questions Are Proving

1. Medical urgency

Recent acute use, clinical instability or a serious diet-sensitive condition creates a reason to act now.

2. Nutrition barrier

The member cannot reliably obtain, prepare or follow the food plan required for the medical condition.

3. Intervention fit

The member can safely receive the service, agrees to participate and is not already receiving a duplicative benefit.
